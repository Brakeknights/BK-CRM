About Brake Knights

We started Brake Knights to do things differently — honest brake repair, delivered to your door, with no games and no fine print.

Our Story

Born from a Better Way to Do Business

Brake Knights was founded by Alex James, an ASE-certified automotive technician who spent years training through trade schools and dealership service bays across Northern Virginia.

After too many years watching customers get hit with hidden fees, deceptive upsells, and inflated labor rates, Alex set out to build something better — a mobile brake repair service built entirely on trust, transparency, and technical excellence.

The premise is simple: your brakes are too important to leave to chance, and your time is too valuable to spend in a waiting room. We bring the shop to you, give you a clear upfront estimate, and do the job right the first time.

Today, Brake Knights has completed over 5,000 brake repairs across 30+ Northern Virginia communities — and the Knight’s Code that Alex started with on day one still governs every service call we make.
